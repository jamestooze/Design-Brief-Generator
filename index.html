Instructions:

In your group use brief above provided to generate multiple alternative concepts (one concept for each member of the group) that address it.

Start by writing a list of simalies and variations for each of your briefs key words (in yellow).

Next, when you have a user in mind consider the context that the device will be used in. What might your user need the device for? How might the form enable this? How might the experience shape the outcome?

Discuss a wide range of ideas and work together to create and communicate the concepts that address the brief.

Each of the concepts should be presented on an A3 piece of paper (landscape), include a concept name, and the brief it was in response to.

Use sketching and annotation to present the concept clearly, maximising the impact and understanding.
